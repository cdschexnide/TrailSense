Devices Tab Sections

  1. Device Summary (3-up cards) — Total unique fingerprints, Known (whitelisted), and Unknown. Quick ratio of identified vs unidentified devices in the period.
  2. New vs Returning (stacked bar chart) — Per-day breakdown showing first-time vs previously-seen devices. Answers: "Am I seeing the same devices or new ones?" Spike in new devices = worth investigating.
  3. Repeat Visitors (ranked list) — Top fingerprints by visit count, each showing:
    • Fingerprint hash with signal type dot (WiFi blue, BLE teal, Cellular purple)
    • Threat level badge
    • First/last seen dates and common hours
    • Mini sparkline showing visit frequency over the period
    Tappable to navigate to full device fingerprint detail.
  4. Device Categories (horizontal bars) — Breakdown by inferred device type: Phones, Unknown, Beacons, Other. Derived from BLE manufacturer data (Apple/Google detection), advertising patterns, and device name heuristics.
  5. Detections by Sensor (grouped bar chart) — Per-sensor comparison of WiFi/BLE/Cellular detection counts. Answers: "Which sensor is seeing the most activity? Is one sensor underperforming?"
  6. Known Device Activity (list) — Whitelisted devices with friendly names, categories, last seen time, and visit count. Answers: "Is my family's regular pattern normal? Did the delivery driver come this week?"
