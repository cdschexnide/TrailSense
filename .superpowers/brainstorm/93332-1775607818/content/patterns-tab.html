Patterns Tab Sections

  1. Activity Heatmap (hour × day-of-week grid) — GitHub-contributions-style heatmap showing detection density per 2-hour block across the week. Instantly reveals: "Saturday nights are hot, Sunday mornings are dead." Custom-built component since no chart library does this well.
  2. Hourly Distribution (24-bar chart) — Total detections bucketed by hour of day. Shows peak and quietest hours. Answers: "What time of day should I pay most attention?"
  3. Day of Week (bar chart) — Total detections per day of week. Busiest day highlighted. Answers: "Which days see the most activity?"
  4. Nighttime Activity (isolated view) — Detections between 10 PM–6 AM only, with count, percentage of total, and trend line. Security-relevant since nighttime activity is inherently more suspicious. Uses the device's time-of-day threat scoring (+20 points for 22:00–06:00).
  5. Anomalies (auto-detected list) — Statistically unusual patterns: spikes above normal baseline, unexpected quiet periods, timing shifts. Computed by comparing current period against historical averages. Each anomaly shows magnitude ("3.2× above normal").
  6. Activity by Sensor (multi-line trend) — Per-sensor detection rate over time. Answers: "Is one sensor seeing more traffic than others? Did something change?"
