Signals Tab Sections

  1. Signal Strength Distribution (histogram) — RSSI distribution across all detections. Shows where most signals land in the -90 to -20 dBm range, with median and peak callouts. Answers: "How close are devices typically getting?"
  2. Proximity Zone Breakdown — Concentric zone visualization showing detection counts per zone (Immediate <5m, Near 5–15m, Far 15–50m, Extreme >50m). Directly from the device's zone classification.
  3. Detection Confidence — Distribution of the device's confidence scores (0–100%). High/Medium/Low buckets with progress bars. Answers: "How reliable are my detections?"
  4. Signal Modality Breakdown — Per-type cards (WiFi, BLE, Cellular) each showing 3 key metrics unique to that modality:
    • WiFi: Channels active, Probe request %, Phantom MAC merges
    • BLE: Phone %, Apple/Google %, Beacon %
    • Cellular: Avg power dBm, Avg burst duration, Noise floor
  5. Cross-Modal Correlations — WiFi↔BLE device links found by the V2 detection engine, plus phantom MAC merges (MAC rotation detection). Shows how many "unique persons" were identified across modalities.
  6. Signal Strength Trend — Multi-line chart showing average RSSI per modality over time. Answers: "Are devices getting closer over the period?"
