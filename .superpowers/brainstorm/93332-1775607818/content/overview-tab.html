Overview Tab Sections

  1. Period Comparison Cards (2×2 grid) — Detections, Unique Devices, Avg Confidence, Closest Approach. Each shows value + % change vs previous period. Color-coded: green = improving, red/amber = needs attention.
  2. Threat Trend (stacked area chart) — Replaces the current single line chart. Shows all 4 threat levels stacked over time so you see both total volume and severity mix. Uses react-native-gifted-charts AreaChart.
  3. Detection Breakdown (horizontal stacked bar) — Replaces the current pie chart. Shows WiFi/BLE/Cellular proportions as a single stacked bar with counts below. More space-efficient than a pie chart.
  4. Highlights (insight cards) — Auto-generated insights: new devices, activity spikes, repeat visitors, anomalies. Each tappable — navigates to the relevant detail tab. This is what makes Overview "insight-driven."
  5. Sensor Health Summary — Per-device status with battery % and uptime. Quick glance at whether your sensors are healthy.
